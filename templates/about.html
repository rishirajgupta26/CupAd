About Us

Learn more about CupAd and our innovative advertising approach

Our Story

CupAd was founded in 2024 with a simple yet innovative idea: transform everyday paper cups into powerful advertising mediums. We recognized that in a world saturated with digital advertisements, physical, tangible media could create a unique and memorable impression.

Our journey began with partnerships with local cafes and quickly expanded to national chains, events, and corporate clients. Today, we're proud to be India's leading cup advertising company, connecting brands with consumers through a medium that's both eco-friendly and effective.

Our Mission

At CupAd, our mission is to revolutionize advertising by creating meaningful connections between brands and consumers through innovative, sustainable, and impactful cup advertising solutions.

We believe that effective advertising doesn't have to be intrusive or environmentally harmful. By utilizing paper cups that are already in use, we provide a platform for brands to communicate their message in a way that's both subtle and powerful.

Our Values

Sustainability

We're committed to eco-friendly practices and use recyclable materials for all our cup advertisements.

Innovation

We constantly explore new ways to make cup advertising more effective and engaging for consumers.

Partnership

We believe in building strong, mutually beneficial relationships with our clients and vendors.

Results

We're dedicated to delivering measurable results and ROI for our clients' advertising campaigns.
